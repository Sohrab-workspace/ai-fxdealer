# News

The list of incoming news is stored in the "News" tab. The news topics are represented as a table and arranged according to the incoming time. The news incoming time, its topic and category are published in the table. This list is updated automatically at incoming of the latest news.

The following commands are available in the context menu:

-   View — view selected news. One can also read news by double-click with the left mouse button on the topic;
-   Category — show/hide the "Category" column;
-   Auto Arrange — automatic arrangement of columns when changing a window size;
-   Grid — show/hide grid to separate columns.
