# Community

The [MQL5.community](https://www.mql5.com/ "MQL5.community") provides unique services to traders and MQL4/MQL5 developer, including the possibility to subscribe to [trading signals](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/signals) provided by professional traders.

![MQL5.community](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/options_community.png "MQL5.community")

Specify your MQL5.community account in order to subscribe to trading signals:

-   Login — your account at MQL5.community.
-   Password — password to the specified account.

<table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable"><ul><li class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">The password is kept on the hard drive in an encrypted form.</span></li><li class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">If you do not have an MQL5.community account, please <a href="https://www.mql5.com/en/auth_register" target="_blank" class="weblink" title="Register at MQL5.community">register</a> and get access to unique opportunities.</span></li></ul></td></tr></tbody></table>

Link "register" opens the window of quick registration of an account at MQL5.community.

![Registration of MQL5.community account](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/mql5community_registration.png "Registration of MQL5.community account")

In this window specify a desired login for your account and email address. Once you click "Register" an account will be created and a message containing password to the account will be sent at the specified email.
