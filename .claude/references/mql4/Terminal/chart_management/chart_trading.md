# Quick Trading

To perform instant trade operations directly on a chart, a special panel is available. To activate it execute the "![One Click Trading](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/chart_oneclick_trading_context.png "One Click Trading") One Click Trading" command in the chart context menu.

The one click trading panel can be shown or hidden by clicking ![One Click Trading](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/chart_oneclick_trading_icon.png "One Click Trading") to the left of the OHLC line.

![One Click Trading](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/chart_oneclick_trading.png "One Click Trading")

Using this panel you can instantly send [market buy and sell orders](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/positions/orders) with a specified volume.
