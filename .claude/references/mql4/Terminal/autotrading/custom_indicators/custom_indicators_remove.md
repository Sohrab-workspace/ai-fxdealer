# Remove

To shut down an indicator, one has to remove it from the chart. At that, its drawing and recalculation of its values will stop. To remove an indicator from the chart, one has to execute its context menu commands of "Delete Indicator" or "Delete Indicator Window", or the [chart context menu](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/chart_management/charts_control) command of "Indicators List — Delete".
