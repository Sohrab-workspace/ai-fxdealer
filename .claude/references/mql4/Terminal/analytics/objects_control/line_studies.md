# Line Studies

Line studies are lines and various geometrical shapes that can be imposed into a price or indicator chart. They include support/resistance lines, trend lines, as well as Fibonacci's, Gann's, and Andrews' tools, etc.

Line studies are collected in the ["Insert" menu](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/overview/main_menu/main_menu_insert) and in the ["Line Studies" toolbar](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/overview/toolbars/toolbars_line_studies) in terminal. To impose an object, one has to press the corresponding toolbar button (the buttons are given in the leftmost column of the table) or execute a menu command.
