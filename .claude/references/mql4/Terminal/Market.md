# Market

Market tab connects trading platform users with MQL4 programs store at [MQL5.community](https://www.mql5.com/en/market). Here they can download for free or buy trading robots, indicators and other applications for the trading terminal.

The upper part of the tab is designed to search the applications.

-   Category — experts, indicators, panels, utilities, etc.
-   Price and novelty — sorting products by price and novelty.
-   Favorites — product marked as favorite.
-   Purchased — [list of products](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/overview/terminal/toolbox_market#purchases) purchased/downloaded by the user;

-   ![Search](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/market_search_icon.png "Search") — the field for searching applications by their name or description. Enter the word into the search field and click ![Search](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/market_search_icon.png "Search").

## Viewing Products

The list of applications displays their cost and rating formed by the users evaluation. The "Free" label means that the application is distributed on a free of charge basis.

Click on the application's logo to view, download or purchase it:

![Viewing an Application](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/toolbox_market_view.png "Viewing an Application")

The upper part contains the toolbar for switching between the application data tabs:

-   ![Back](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/toolbox_market_back_button.png "Back") — back to the applications list.
-   Application — a detailed description of the application can be viewed here.
-   Screenshots — application screenshots can be viewed here.
-   Reviews — users' reviews about the application can be viewed here. The tab is not displayed, in case there are no reviews.

## Purchasing Products Without Signing Up for MQL5.community [#](market#no_registration)

If you do not have an [MQL5.community](https://www.mql5.com) account yet, you can still buy a Market product. Select the appropriate payment system and make a payment as described above.

![Purchasing a product with no MQL5.community account](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/market_buy_no_registration.png "Purchasing a product with no MQL5.community account")

We strongly recommend that you [sign up for MQL5.community](https://www.mql5.com/en/auth_register) after making a purchase. This will allow you to:

-   [Install](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/market#redownload) the product on other computers within the available number of activations
-   Receive free [product updates](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/market#update)

The account should be registered directly from the terminal. Click [Register MQL5.community account](https://www.mql5.com/en/auth_register) in the upper part of the Market window. After the registration is complete, the purchase is bound to the new account. You can see all your payments and purchases in your profile on the MQL5.com website.

## Updating applications [#](market#update)

From time to time sellers may release updated versions of their products to increase reliability and extend functionality.

<table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable"><ul><li class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">All updates of previously purchased products are free of charge.</span></li><li class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">Updates of products become available in the client terminal with a short delay after they are published in the <a href="https://www.mql5.com/en/market" target="_blank" class="weblink" title="MQL5.community Market">Market on MQL5.community</a>.</span></li></ul></td></tr></tbody></table>

To check whether new versions of your previously purchased or downloaded products are available, go to the "Purchased" tab.

![Updating products](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/market_update.png "Updating products")

If a new version of a product is available, you will see the corresponding message against it as well as the "Update" button (or the "Update demo" button for demo version of paid products).

Once this button is pressed, the new version will be downloaded. The new file replaces the previous one. Thus if need, save the old version of the file under a different name or outside of the directory \[terminal data folder\]\\MQL4\\Market\\.

## Renting Products [#](market#rent)

The Market products can be rented for 1, 3, 6 or 12 months. For buyers, the rent is another opportunity to assess a product before buying a full license. Unlike demo versions that can be launched only in the strategy tester, rented products have no limitations except for validity period.

<table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable"><p class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">Rental period and fee, as well as the very possibility to rent are set by product developers. Therefore, some products may be unavailable for rent.</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table>

Rental fee and period are displayed under a product logo:

![Rent](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/market_rent.png "Rent")

Click Rent and select the rental period. As in the case of the [full versions](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/market#purchases), you can pay for your purchase via your MQL5.community account or using one of the available payment systems. Besides, you can rent a product [without signing up for MQL5.community](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/market#no_registration).

<table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable"><p class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">After the period expires, rented products stop their operation automatically. For example, a rented trading robot is automatically removed from a chart. So, be careful not to leave your positions unattended if they have been managed by a rented Expert Advisor.</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table>

The following entry is periodically displayed in the terminal [journal](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/overview/terminal/terminal_journal) one day before the end of the rental period:

<table class="table" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="5" border="1"><tbody><tr class="table"><td class="table"><p class="p_CodeExample"><span class="f_CodeExample">Licence&nbsp;of&nbsp;'product.ex5'&nbsp;expires&nbsp;on&nbsp;02.03.2015.&nbsp;Please&nbsp;renew&nbsp;the&nbsp;license,&nbsp;otherwise&nbsp;the&nbsp;program&nbsp;will&nbsp;be&nbsp;stopped</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table>

In order to renew a rental period or buy a full version, move to Purchased section.

![Rent renewal](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/market_rent_prolong.png "Rent renewal")

Rental period expiration date is shown to the right of a product name. The current period expiration time is considered to be the beginning of the renewed rental period. Thus, you can renew the rent in advance without losing the time remaining till the current period expires.

If you want to purchase a full version, click Buy. In this case, you pay the full cost of a product. Previously paid rental fees are not considered.

## Purchased/Downloads [#](market#purchases)

"Purchased" tab displays all the products downloaded and purchased using the current [MQL5.community account](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/setup/settings_mql5community). If MQL5.community account data is not specified in the terminal settings, the tab name changes to "Downloads". It displays all downloads and purchases made in the current client terminal regardless of the account.

![Purchased/Downloads](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/img/toolbox_market_purchases.png "Purchased/Downloads")

The following information is displayed here:

-   Name — product name. Demo versions of products are marked with the Demo label.
-   Category — product category, such as Experts, Indicators etc. If you click on the product category, it will be selected in the ["Navigator"](/en/docs/mt4/terminal/overview/navigator) window.
-   Price — product cost in USD.
-   Date — product download/purchase date.
