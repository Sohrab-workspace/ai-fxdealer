# Trading platform video guides

We have prepared small videos for you. They show the main features of the trading platform and explain how to use its unique services.

## MetaTrader 5 trading platform overview

Technical analysis, advanced trading functions, Market of trading robots and indicators, Trading signals and Virtual hosting - find out what your trading platform is capable of.

          

## Where can you get a trading robot or an indicator

The trading platform features a great amount of popular technical indicators. However, you can receive even more tools for your trading. A large number of additional custom indicators can be accessed straight from the trading platform.

          

## Buying trading robots and indicators in the Market

Market is the largest store of trading robots and indicators for the trading platform. In addition to purchasing, you can download a variety of free useful applications here. You can check trading robots and indicators for free in the strategy tester before buying.

          

## Trading signals and copy trading

A set of videos on trading signals about choosing a signals provider, analyzing their trades, subscribing and, finally, receiving reports on all subscriptions.

          

## Virtual hosting and round-the-clock trading

A set of videos on virtual hosting about renting hosting directly from the trading platform, preparing a trading environment for migrating to a server and monitoring the operation of trading robots and indicators.
