# Welcome to the section of Documentation for the MetaTrader 4 platform

Here you can find the most actual documentation for all components of the platform.

## Managers

-   [**Administrator terminal**](/en/docs/mt4/administrator)  
    It is intended for controlling operation of servers and other components of the platform, managing settings of groups of users, manager permissions, financial instruments and data feeds, setting up operation of access servers, managing quotes database, processing logs of server functioning, performing synchronization of history data of charts, restarting data feeds and servers, setting up automatic updates of programs, regulating working time, controlling function of database reserving.
-   [**Manager terminal**](/en/docs/mt4/manager)  
    It is intended for managing accounts and positions of clients, opening new accounts, processing clients' trade requests, analyzing aggregate positions of clients and managing company's own positions, receiving full information about clients' operations, sending quotes to the data thread on the server, generating various types of statements, sending and receiving messages over the internal mailing system, publishing news in client terminals.

## Clients

-   [**Client terminal**](/en/docs/mt4/terminal)  
    The terminal provides all the necessary tools needed to ensure full-fledged and flexible trading activities. The powerful trading arsenal is available with a click of the mouse: trade operations, alerts, technical analysis, fundamental analysis and automated trading programs.  
    
-   **[MetaEditor](/en/docs/mt4/metaeditor)**  
    The MetaEditor works with MQL4 program source codes and compiles them into executable files. As the trading robots editor and Trading Platform are interconnected, the compiled forex robots generated become automatically available in MetaTrader 4 and can be instantly executed.  
    
-   [**MultiTerminal**](/en/docs/mt4/multiterminal)  
    The MetaTrader 4 MultiTerminal is designed for managing multiple accounts simultaneously. It is very useful for investors' accounts managers and for traders working with multiple accounts simultaneously.
-   [**WebTerminal**](/en/docs/mt4/administrator/web_terminal)  
    The MetaTrader 4 WebTerminal allows trading in the financial markets via a web browser. It works in all operating systems and browsers requiring no additional software. All transmitted data is securely encrypted.
