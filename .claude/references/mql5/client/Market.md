# Market

[Market](https://www.mql5.com/en/market "Market") is the largest store of trading robots and indicators for the trading platform. Here you can [purchase](/en/docs/mt5/client/market_buy) or download for free various trading applications. Trading robots and indicators can be tested for free before purchasing.

Applications from the Market are also available for [rent](/en/docs/mt5/client/market_rent). You receive a full-featured product with a limited validity period, but at a lower cost.

If you are a professional trader and programmer, you are welcome to [offer your products](/en/docs/mt5/client/market_sell) on the Market. You access to an audience of millions traders and you do not have to worry about payments or licensing: payments are transferred through the [MQL5.community](https://www.mql5.com/ "MQL5.community") payment system, while products are encrypted and tied to buyers' computers.

## How to Find the Right Application in the Market?

The Market features a well-developed product filtration and sorting systems. Each product has a detailed description and screenshots. Trading robots and indicators have demo versions. Watch our video to know how to use all these options and make the right choice.

          

## Free Testing of Expert Advisors and Indicators before Purchase

Before making a purchase it is recommended to test desired trading robots and indicators. It is an easy and free-of-charge operation that gives you more confidence in a product. Watch the video for details.

          

## How to Purchase a Trading Robot or an Indicator from the Market?

Any trader can find thousands of trading robots, indicators, financial magazines and books in the Market. You can purchase them straight from the platform. Watch the video to find out the details.

          

## How to Update, Renew the Rental Period and Reinstall Products

All your products are tied to your MQL5.community account or to your computer and are always at hand. You will always know about updates, will be able to renew the rent or download a previously purchased product. Watch the video to find out how easy that is.
