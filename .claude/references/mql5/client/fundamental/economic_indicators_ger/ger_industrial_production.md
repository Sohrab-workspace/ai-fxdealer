# Industrial Production

This indicator shows the level of change in industrial production in the country. The indicator takes into account the manufacturing and mining industry, forestry and electricity generation.

-   Release Frequency: monthly.
-   Release Schedule: 11:00 GMT, 4-7th of the month following the reporting period.
