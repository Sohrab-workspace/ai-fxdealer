# International Transactions in Securities

The indicator shows the difference between the [inflow of foreign capital](https://www.mql5.com/en/economic-calendar/canada/foreign-securities-purchases) and [outflows from the country](https://www.mql5.com/en/economic-calendar/canada/foreign-securities-purchases-by-canadians). The indicator helps to evaluate the demand of foreigners for Canadian investments and the Canadian dollar. A positive balance reveals that more capital is entering Canada than leaving. This is a signal of the currency growth. A negative balance reflects the worsening economic trends and the fall of the Canadian dollar rate.

-   Release Frequency: monthly.
-   Release Schedule: 08:30 EST, two months after the reporting period.
-   Source: Statistics Canada.
