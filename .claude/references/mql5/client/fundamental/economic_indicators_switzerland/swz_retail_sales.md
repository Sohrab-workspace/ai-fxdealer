# Retail Sales

This is the [index of change in retail sales](https://www.mql5.com/en/economic-calendar/switzerland/retail-sales-yy). This is an important indicator of consumer spendings, and is used for calculating the [Consumer Price Index](/en/docs/mt5/client/fundamental/economic_indicators_switzerland/swz_consumer_price_index). Is measured as a percentage.

-   Release Frequency: monthly.
-   Release Schedule: 07:15 GMT.
-   Source: Swiss Federal Statistical Office.
