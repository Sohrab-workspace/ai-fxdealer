# Capital and Financial Account

This indicator is a part of [Balance of Payments](/en/docs/mt5/client/fundamental/economic_indicators_euro/euro_balance_of_payments). The Capital and Financial Account is the ratio of the movement of public and private funds into and out of a country, received and issued credits and results of transactions on government reserves.

-   Release Frequency: monthly.
-   Release Schedule: 9:00 GMT, about 50 days after the reporting month.
-   Source: Eurostat.
