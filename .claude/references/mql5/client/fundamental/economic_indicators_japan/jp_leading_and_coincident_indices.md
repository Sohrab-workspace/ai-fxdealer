# Leading and Coincident Indices of Business Conditions

The [index of leading indicators](https://www.mql5.com/en/economic-calendar/japan/leading-index) is a weighted average of 13 major indicators. It is used for determining the future state of the economy. The index of coincident indicators is composed of 11 indicators and is used for assessing the current state of the economy (the 50% level of the indicator is "zero"). They have little effect on the market.

-   Release Frequency: monthly.
-   Release Schedule: 05:00 GMT, two months after the reporting period.
-   Source: Economic and Social Research Institute.
