# Chart Print

The trading platform provides chart printing tools. A chart can be printed in black and white or in color. For color print, enable the "Color print" option in the [platform settings](/en/docs/mt5/client/settings#charts). In this case charts are printed out in color if the printing device allows it. Otherwise charts are printed in black and white.

Before printing you may sometimes need to set up your printer. Click "Print Setup" in the [File](/en/docs/mt5/client/interface) menu. To make sure that all the required elements fit into the printable area, click "![Print Preview](/en/docs/mt5/client/img/print_preview_icon.png "Print Preview") Print Preview" in the same menu or on the [Standard](/en/docs/mt5/client/interface) toolbar.

To print a chart, click "![Print](/en/docs/mt5/client/img/print_icon.png "Print") Print" in the [File](/en/docs/mt5/client/interface) menu or the [Standard](/en/docs/mt5/client/interface) toolbar, or press "Ctrl+P".
