# Deleted Charts

Sometimes you may want to delete charts that you do not need anymore. Select "Close" in the [File](/en/docs/mt5/client/interface) menu or press "Ctrl+F4".

Templates of deleted charts are saved and can be used later. For example, if a chart has been deleted accidentally, it can be restored. Click "Open Deleted" in the [File](/en/docs/mt5/client/interface) menu and select a symbol from the list. The chart window of the selected symbol is fully restored with all its settings and objects after that.

To permanently delete saved charts, click "Delete" in the "Open Deleted" menu.
