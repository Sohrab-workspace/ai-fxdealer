# Shapes

Various shapes can be applied to a price or indicator chart using the "Objects — Shapes" items of the ["Insert"](/en/docs/mt5/client/interface) menu or the ["Line Studies"](/en/docs/mt5/client/interface) toolbar. Shapes serve to highlight important areas in a chart. The following shapes are available in the platform:
