# Trading Platform — User Manual

The Trading Platform is the trader's working tool, providing all the necessary features for a successful online trading. It includes [trading](/en/docs/mt5/client/trading), technical [analysis of prices](/en/docs/mt5/client/charts_analysis) and [fundamental analysis](/en/docs/mt5/client/fundamental), [automated trading](/en/docs/mt5/client/algotrading) and trading from [mobile devices](/en/docs/mt5/client/mobile_trading). In addition to Forex symbols, options futures and stocks can be traded from the platform.
