<table class="container" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0" border="0"><tbody><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h1>Indicators for Technical Analysis</h1><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">A technical indicator is a mathematical transformation of a security price and/or volumes aimed at forecasting future price changes. Decisions about how and when to open or close positions can be made on the basis of signals produced by technical indicators. Indicators can be divided into two groups based on their functional properties: trend indicators and oscillators. Trend indicators help to assess the price direction and detect the reversal moments synchronously or with a delay. Oscillators help to find reversal points in advance or synchronously.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">MetaTrader 5 for Android provides 30 indicators. They are divided into the following groups:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol"><a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators/trend_indicators" class="topiclink">Trend Indicators</a></span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol"><a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators/oscillators" class="topiclink">Oscillators</a></span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol"><a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators/volume_indicators" class="topiclink">Volume Indicators</a></span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol"><a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators/bw_indicators" class="topiclink">Bill Williams' Indicators</a></span></li></ul><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">A technical indicator can be drawn in a separate indicator window with its own vertical scale (for example, <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators/oscillators/macd" class="topiclink">MACD</a>) or applied directly to a price chart (like <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators/trend_indicators/moving_average" class="topiclink">Moving Average</a>). Each indicator has a specific set of <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators#settings" class="topiclink">parameters</a>.</span></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Managing Indicators" title="Managing Indicators" width="322" height="636" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/indicator_management.png"></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><h2><a name="add" class="hmanchor"></a>Adding Indicators <a class="anchor" href="indicators#add">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Tap <img class="help" alt="Indicators" title="Indicators" width="24" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/chart_indicators_button.png"> on the top panel of the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart" class="topiclink">chart</a> or in the radial menu. If the chart has no indicators, a list of available indicators will open. The selected indicator will be applied to the active chart window.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Otherwise, the list of chart windows will open. Tap <img class="help" alt="Add an indicator" title="Add an indicator" width="20" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/add_icon.png"> next to the required window to go to the list of available indicators.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">If the selected indicator should be plotted in a separate window (such as, for example, <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators/oscillators/macd" class="topiclink">MACD</a>), the window will be created automatically. Once an additional window is created, you can add indicators to this window.</span></p><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="List of Indicators" title="List of Indicators" width="650" height="636" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/indicator_list.png"></p><div><div class="parent-table"><table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable"><p class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">The total number of indicators on all charts cannot exceed 32.</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table></div></div></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Indicator settings" title="Indicator settings" width="322" height="636" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/indicator_settings.png"></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h2><a name="settings" class="hmanchor"></a>Indicator Settings <a class="anchor" href="indicators#settings">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Each indicator has a specific set of parameters. Parameters are set when an indicator is attached to the chart.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">One of the common parameters is "Apply to". Indicators can be plotted based on price data and derivatives thereof (Median Price, Typical Price, Weighted Close), as well as on the basis of other indicators. For example, a <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators/trend_indicators/moving_average" class="topiclink">Moving Average</a> can be constructed based on <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators/oscillators/macd" class="topiclink">MACD</a> values. To do this, first plot the MACD indicator, and then in its window <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators#add" class="topiclink">add</a> MA, selecting in the "Apply to" field of its settings the "Previous indicator's data" option. If you select "First indicator's data", MA will be constructed on the basis of the very first indicator, which is not necessarily the MACD. Nine variants of indicator construction are available:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Close </span><span class="f_ol">— based on close prices.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Open</span><span class="f_ol"> — based on open prices.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">High</span><span class="f_ol"> — based on High prices.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Low</span><span class="f_ol"> — based on Low prices.</span></li></ul></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Median Price (HL/2)</span><span class="f_ol"> — based on the median price: (High + Low)/2.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Typical Price (HLC/3)</span><span class="f_ol"> — based on the typical price: (High + Low + Close)/3.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Weighted Close (HLCC/4)</span><span class="f_ol"> — based on the average weighted close price: (High + Low + 2*Close)/4.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">First indicator's data</span><span class="f_ol"> — based on the values of the first applied indicator. The option of using the data of the first indicator is only available for indicators in a separate window, because in the main chart window the first indicator is the price.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Previous indicator's data</span><span class="f_ol"> — based on the values of the previous indicator.</span></li></ul><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">In addition to this parameter, you can set the Period, Method, Shift and many other parameters of indicators.</span></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h2><a name="delete" class="hmanchor"></a>Removing Indicators <a class="anchor" href="indicators#delete">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To delete indicators, tap <img class="help" alt="Delete" title="Delete" width="15" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/delete_button.png"> at the top of the window. Then tick off all the indicators that you want to delete, and once again tap <img class="help" alt="Delete" title="Delete" width="15" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/delete_button.png">.</span></p><h2>Modifying Indicators</h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To open the settings of a previously launched indicator, tap <img class="help" alt="Indicators" title="Indicators" width="24" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/chart_indicators_button.png">and select this indicator from the list.</span></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Removing an indicator" title="Removing an indicator" width="322" height="636" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/indicators_delete.png"></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Hiding Indicators" title="Hiding Indicators" width="322" height="654" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/indicator_hide.png"></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h2><a name="hide" class="hmanchor"></a>Hiding Indicators <a class="anchor" href="indicators#hide">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To quickly view a "clean" chart without deleting the previously added indicators, tap </span><img class="help" alt="Hide All" title="Hide All" width="30" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/hide_button.png"><span class="f_ImageCaption"> </span><span class="f_txt">at the top of the indicator list. You can also hide individual indicators by tapping the similar buttons next to their name.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Also, in <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/chart/indicators#settings" class="topiclink">indicator settings</a>, you can additionally specify the timeframes on which the indicators will be displayed.</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table>
