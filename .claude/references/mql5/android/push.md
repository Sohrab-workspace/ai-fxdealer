<table class="container" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0" border="0"><tbody><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h1>Push notifications</h1><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">MetaTrader 5 for Android supports <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/push" class="topiclink">push notifications</a>. Push notifications are short text messages that can be sent to your device from the PC version of the trading platform, and from various services of the <a href="https://www.mql5.com/" target="_blank" class="weblink" title="MQL5.community">MQL5.community</a>.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Push notifications are very important. It allows traders to keep abreast of the latest updates. An Expert Advisor running in a desktop trading platform can notify a trader about the emergence of a new signal. The trader receives the signal to the mobile device and can perform a trading operation through the MetaTrader 5 for Android.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Push notifications are received by the device, regardless of whether the MetaTrader 5 application is running or not. All that is needed is the mobile Internet.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">During installation of MetaTrader 5 for Android, a unique identifier called <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/messages#my_id" class="topiclink">MetaQuotes ID</a> is assigned to each user. This identifier is used like a phone number. Knowing it, various applications can send messages to the user.</span></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="The History of Messages" title="The History of Messages" width="324" height="552" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/message_history.png"></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Push messages are displayed in the notification center of your device. The entire history of push notifications is stored in MetaTrader 5 for Android and can be viewed at any time.</span></p><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Messages in the notification center" title="Messages in the notification center" width="583" height="512" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/messages_new_notify.png"></p><div><div class="parent-table"><table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable"><p class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten"><a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/messages#my_id" class="topiclink">Specify MetaQuotes ID</a> in your user profile at MQL5.community and in the trading platform in order to receive notifications on your mobile device.</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table></div></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
