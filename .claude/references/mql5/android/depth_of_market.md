In this case, the Depth of Market displays price levels calculated based on the Bid and Ask prices using the price change step.</span></li></ul></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Depth of Market" title="Depth of Market" width="322" height="656" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/depth_of_market.png"></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">For more information about prices in the Depth of Market, please see the <a href="https://www.metatrader5.com/en/terminal/help/trading_advanced/price_data" target="_blank" class="weblink">Price Data</a> section.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To open the Depth of Market window of a financial instrument, click "Depth of Market" in its context menu in <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/quotes" class="topiclink">"Quotes"</a>.</span></p><div><div class="parent-table"><table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable"><p class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">The number of bids and offers displayed in the DOM is determined by the symbol parameters set by the broker.</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table></div></div></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Depth of Market" title="Depth of Market" width="322" height="656" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/depth_of_market.png"></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h2><a name="market" class="hmanchor"></a>Market Operations <a class="anchor" href="depth_of_market#market">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">A market operation is buying/selling a financial instrument at the best price currently offered on the market. For example:</span></li></ul><ul><li class="p_ol_2lev"><span class="f_ol_2lev" style="font-weight: bold;">BL 10</span><span class="f_ol_2lev"> — Buy Limit order of 10 lots;</span></li><li lot;</span></li><li class="p_ol_2lev"><span class="f_ol_2lev" style="font-weight: bold;">tp 2</span><span class="f_ol_2lev"> — Take Profit of a position of 1 lot.</span></li></ul><p
