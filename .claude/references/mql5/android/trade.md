<table class="container" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0" border="0"><tbody><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h1>How To Trade</h1><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The basis of the profit while working in the financial markets is a simple rule: buy cheap and sell dear. One of the main functions of the MetaTrader 5 mobile platform for Android is providing a user with wide opportunities to perform buy and sell operations.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The "Trade" tab features the current state of a trade account, as well as current <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/trade/open_positions" class="topiclink">positions</a> and <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/trade/pending_place" class="topiclink">pending orders</a>. To switch to this tab, use the application side panel that opens at a tap on </span><img class="help" alt="Open the side panel" title="Open the side panel" width="29" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/side_panel_button.png"><span class="f_txt">.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To go to placing of a <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/trade/open_positions" class="topiclink">new order</a>, tap on <img class="help" alt="New order" title="New order" width="16" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/new_order_button.png"> on the top panel.</span></p><h2><a name="account_state" class="hmanchor"></a>Account state <a class="anchor" href="trade#account_state">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The current state of a trade account is shown at the top of the tab:</span></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="How To Trade" title="How To Trade" width="322" height="636" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/trade.png"></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Profit, currency </span><span class="f_ol">— the total returns of the current trade positions and the account deposit currency.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Balance</span><span class="f_ol"> — money on the account, not accounting for the results of currently open positions (deposit);</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Credit</span><span class="f_ol"> — amount of funds provided to a trader by a broker as a loan;</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Assets</span><span class="f_ol"> — the current value of purchased financial instruments (of long positions) defined in trader's deposit currency. The value is determined dynamically based on the price of the latest symbol deal taking into account the liquidity margin rate. In fact, the amount of assets is equivalent to the amount of money that the trader would receive in case of immediate closure of long positions. This rate is used on exchange markets.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Liabilities</span><span class="f_ol"> — obligations on current short positions calculated as the value of these positions at the current market price. In fact, the amount of liabilities is equivalent to the amount of money that the trader would pay in case of immediate closure of short positions.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Commission </span><span class="f_ol">— commission by orders and positions accumulated during a day/month. Depending on conditions of collection of commission (determined by broker), a preliminary calculation of commission is performed during a day or a month. The corresponding amount of assets is blocked on the account and its value is shown in this field. Final commission calculation is performed at the end of a day/month, and the appropriate sum is withdrawn from the account by the balance operation (it is displayed as a separate deal on the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/android/history" class="topiclink">History</a> tab), while the blocked amount is unlocked. In case commission is charged immediately during a deal, its value is shown in the "Commission" field on the history tab.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Blocked</span><span class="f_ol"> — under certain trading conditions (determined by a broker), a profit fixed during a day cannot be used to perform trade operations (not included in the free margin). This blocked profit is displayed in the "Blocked" field. At the end of the trading day, this profit is unblocked and deposited to the account balance.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Equity</span><span class="f_ol"> — equity is calculated as Balance + Credit - Commission +/- Floating profit/loss - Blocked;</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Margin</span><span class="f_ol"> — money required to cover open positions and pending orders;</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Free Margin</span><span class="f_ol"> — the free amount of money that can be used to open positions. It is calculated as Equity - Margin. Depending on the trading conditions (defined by a broker), the equity value may or may not consider: floating profit, floating loss or floating profit and floating loss together;</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Margin Level</span><span class="f_ol"> — percentage of the account equity to the margin volume (Equity / Margin * 100);</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Total of deals</span><span class="f_ol"> — total financial result of all open positions.</span></li></ul><div><div class="parent-table"><table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable"><p class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">If the account gets "Margin Call", the margin fields are highlighted in red.</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table></div></div></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Position/order management commands" title="Position/order management commands" width="322" height="636" src="/en/docs/mt5/android/img/position_menu.png"></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h2>Trading Commands</h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To quickly modify or close a position or order, swipe on its row from right to left.
