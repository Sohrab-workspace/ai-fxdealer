## The order of end-of-day/month service operations

The order of operations depends on the report generation mode: at the end or at the beginning of the day ([IMTConServerTrade::EnOvernightMode](/en/docs/mt5/api/config_network/imtconservertrade/imtconservertrade_enum#enovernightmode)). The performed actions also depend on whether the end of the trading day (as determined by the schedule [IMTConServerTrade::OvernightDays](/en/docs/mt5/api/config_network/imtconservertrade/imtconservertrade_overnightdays)) and end of month (as determined by the actual end of month) fall on this day.
