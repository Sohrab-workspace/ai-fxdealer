# Trading Operations

The Manager terminal allows you to conduct any trade operations on clients' accounts: [place](/en/docs/mt5/manager/orders#place) and [activate pending orders](/en/docs/mt5/manager/orders#activate), [open](/en/docs/mt5/manager/positions#open) and [close positions](/en/docs/mt5/manager/positions#close), etc. In case of appropriate permissions, it is possible to [edit orders, trades and positions parameters](/en/docs/mt5/manager/edit_trades) in the trade server databases. Managers always have the list of all open positions and pending orders of their clients.

![Trading functions in the Manager terminal](/en/docs/mt5/manager/img/trading.png "Trading functions in the Manager terminal")

The terminal features multiple additional tools to perform trade operations:

-   [Market Watch](/en/docs/mt5/manager/market_watch) with real-time quotes
-   [Depth of Market](/en/docs/mt5/manager/depth_of_market) (level 2) with requests that are the closest ones to the market
-   [Economic calendar](/en/docs/mt5/manager/economic_calendar) providing data on releases of important indices affecting quotes
-   [Trading notifications](/en/docs/mt5/manager/trade_alerts) to avoid missing important price levels
