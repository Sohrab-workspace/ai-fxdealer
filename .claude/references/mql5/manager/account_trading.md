# Trading Operations

The Manager terminal allows you to conduct any trade operations on accounts on behalf of clients: put pending orders, open and close positions, etc. To perform operations, open the Trade tab.

![Conducting trade operations on the account on behalf of the client](/en/docs/mt5/manager/img/account_trade.png "Conducting trade operations on the account on behalf of the client")

Detailed information about conducting trade operations is available in separate sections:

-   [Opening positions](/en/docs/mt5/manager/positions#open)
-   [Editing positions](/en/docs/mt5/manager/positions#modify)
-   [Closing positions](/en/docs/mt5/manager/positions#close)
-   [Closing a position by an opposite one](/en/docs/mt5/manager/positions#closeby)
-   [Placing pending orders](/en/docs/mt5/manager/orders#place)
-   [Modification and deleting of pending orders](/en/docs/mt5/manager/orders#modify_delete)
-   [Activation of pending orders](/en/docs/mt5/manager/orders#activate)

Besides, if you have appropriate permissions, you can [edit orders, trades and positions parameters](/en/docs/mt5/manager/edit_trades) in the trade server databases.
