# Foniva

Foniva is a Turkish platform that provides fast and high-quality SMS messaging services to subscribers throughout the country. To connect to the service, request an account on the [provider's website](https://www.foniva.com.tr/).

Then, go to your personal account at [https://sms.foniva.com.tr/login](https://sms.foniva.com.tr/login) and specify your login details. Next, click on your account name and go to the "API Settings" section. The section contains data for configuring the provider on the MetaTrader 5 side:

![Foniva provider configuration settings are available in your personal account](/en/docs/mt5/platform/img/messengers_foniva.png "Foniva provider configuration settings are available in your personal account")

[Create an SMS gateway configuration](/en/docs/mt5/platform/administration/integration/integration_sms) on the platform side and specify the following parameters:

-   Sender — the name that will be indicated as the message sender. This is a required field. The name should be discussed with the provider when you open your account.
-   Login — login for connection from the personal account.
-   Password — password for connection from the personal account.
-   Currency rate — Turkish lira to US dollar exchange rate to display cost statistics.

## Other Settings

All other settings are standard and no different from those of other SMS gateways. You can manage the provider's availability for [countries](/en/docs/mt5/platform/administration/integration/integration_sms#country) and [groups](/en/docs/mt5/platform/administration/integration/integration_sms#group), as well as customize [message templates](/en/docs/mt5/platform/administration/integration/integration_sms#template).
