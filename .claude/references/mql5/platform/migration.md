# Migration from MetaTrader 4 to MetaTrader 5

MetaTrader 5 is the next generation of the most popular MetaTrader 4 trading platform. The platform allows connection to a variety of markets, trading Forex and exchanges, transactions with currencies, futures, stocks, bonds and other instruments. MetaTrader 5 supports two position accounting systems  hedging (similar to MetaTrader 4 one) and netting for exchanges. Distributed architecture, services for traders, advanced trading and analytical functions, rich integration capabilities - all this and more is available in the fifth generation platform.

We have done our best to ensure the simplicity and convenience of migration to the modern multi-asset MetaTrader 5 platform. Transferring trading and client databases, as well as group and symbol settings is performed automatically via the network. The general platform settings are similar, and it is quite easy to compare them.
