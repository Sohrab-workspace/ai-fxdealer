# Data Feeds

Data feeds enable receipt of quotes and news in the online trading platform. They transmit information to [the history server](/en/docs/mt5/platform/components/history_server), from which the are translated to [access points](/en/docs/mt5/platform/administration/admin_network/network_add_edit/network_access_server) (data centers) and terminals. The trading platform includes several data feeds that enable receiving of quotes and news from the most popular feed providers:

-   [DJ News Feeder](/en/docs/mt5/platform/components/datafeeds/djnewsfeeder)
-   [IQFeeder](/en/docs/mt5/platform/components/datafeeds/iqfeeder)

Data feeds are executable filed (with extension \*.exe) that are run as separate processes. These executable files must be located in the datafeed folder of [the history server](/en/docs/mt5/platform/components/history_server/history_server_structure#datafeed).

After that refresh configurations in the administrator terminal by pressing "<img class="help" alt="Refresh configuration" title="Refresh configuration" width="15" height="15" src="/en/docs/mt5/platform/img/refresh_configuration_button.png"> Refresh configuration".</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table>

In order to use a data feed, connection to the feed provider server needs to be established. Data delivery from a third party vendor is implemented based on a special agreement.
