<table class="container" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0" border="0"><tbody><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h1>How to Trade</h1><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The simple basis rule for profiting in financial markets is 'buy cheap and sell dear'. One of the main functions of the MetaTrader 5 mobile platform for iPhone is providing a user with wide opportunities to perform buy and sell operations.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The Trade tab features the current state of a trade account, as well as current <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/open_positions" class="topiclink">positions</a> and <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/pending_place" class="topiclink">pending orders</a>. To switch to this tab, use the bottom panel of the application.</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol">To perform a <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/open_positions" class="topiclink">trading operation</a>, tap <img class="help" alt="New Order" title="New Order" width="20" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/new_order_button.png">.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol">For quick <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/settings_accounts/payments" class="topiclink">depositing or withdrawing</a>, tap <img class="help" alt="Deposit/Withdraw" title="Deposit/Withdraw" width="28" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/deposit_withdrawal_button.png">.</span></li></ul><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol">To open the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/bulk_operations" class="topiclink">bulk operations menu</a>, tap <img class="help" alt="Bulk Operations" title="Bulk Operations" width="28" height="6" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/bulk_menu.png"> in the header of the positions or orders section.</span></li></ul><h2><a name="account_state" class="hmanchor"></a>Account State <a class="anchor" href="trade#account_state">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The current state of a trade account is shown at the top of the tab:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Profit, currency </span><span class="f_ol">— the total result of the current trade positions and the account deposit currency.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Balance</span><span class="f_ol"> — money on the account, not taking into account the results of currently open positions (deposit).</span></li></ul></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="How To Trade" title="How To Trade" width="324" height="650" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/trade.png"></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Credit</span><span class="f_ol"> — amount of funds provided to a trader by a broker as a loan.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Assets</span><span class="f_ol"> — the current value of purchased financial instruments (of long positions) defined in trader's deposit currency. The value is determined dynamically based on the price of the latest symbol deal taking into account the liquidity margin rate. In fact, the amount of assets is equivalent to the amount of money that the trader would receive in case of immediate closure of long positions. This rate is used on exchange markets.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Liabilities</span><span class="f_ol"> — obligations on current short positions calculated as the value of these positions at the current market price. In fact, the amount of liabilities is equivalent to the amount of money that the trader would pay in case of immediate closure of short positions.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Commission </span><span class="f_ol">— commission by orders and positions accumulated during a day/month. Depending on conditions of collection of commission (determined by broker), a preliminary calculation of commission is performed during a day or a month. The corresponding amount of assets is blocked on the account and its value is shown in this field. Final commission calculation is performed at the end of a day/month and the appropriate sum is withdrawn from the account by the balance operation (which is displayed as a separate deal in the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/history" class="topiclink">History</a> tab), and the frozen amount is unblocked.</span><br><span class="f_ol">In case commission is charged immediately during execution of a deal, its value is shown in the Commission field of deals in the History tab.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Blocked</span><span class="f_ol"> — under certain trading conditions (determined by a broker), a profit fixed during a day cannot be used to perform trade operations (not included in the free margin). This blocked profit is displayed in the "Blocked" field. At the end of the trading day, this profit is unblocked and deposited to the account balance.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Equity</span><span class="f_ol"> — equity is calculated as Balance + Credit - Commission +/- Floating profit/loss - Blocked.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Margin</span><span class="f_ol"> — money required to cover open positions and pending orders.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Free Margin</span><span class="f_ol"> — the free amount of money that can be used to open positions. It is calculated as Equity - Margin. Depending on the trading conditions (defined by a broker), the equity value may or may not consider: floating profit, floating loss or floating profit and floating loss together.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Margin Level</span><span class="f_ol"> — percentage of the account equity to the margin volume (Equity / Margin * 100).</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Total of deals</span><span class="f_ol"> — total financial result of all open positions.</span></li></ul></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Position/order management commands" title="Position/order management commands" width="324" height="648" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/position_menu.png"></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h2>Trading Commands</h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To quickly modify or close a position or order, swipe on its row from right to left. This will open a menu of commands:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><img class="help" alt="Context menu" title="Context menu" width="20" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/position_menu_context.png"><span class="f_ol"> — open a <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade#context" class="topiclink">context menu</a> with additional commands.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><img class="help" alt="position_menu_modify" width="20" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/position_menu_modify.png"><span class="f_ol"> — <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/change_positions" class="topiclink">modify position</a>/<a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/pending_change#modify" class="topiclink">modify order</a>.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><img class="help" alt="position_menu_close" width="20" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/position_menu_close.png"><span class="f_ol"> — <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/open_positions" class="topiclink">close position</a>/<a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/pending_change#delete" class="topiclink">delete order</a>.</span></li></ul><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">By dragging the menu further to the left, you can immediately switch to order closing or canceling screen.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The same commands are available in the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade#context" class="topiclink">context menu</a> of positions and orders. To open the context menu, tap on the trading operation line in the list.</span></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><h2><a name="position" class="hmanchor"></a>Positions <a class="anchor" href="trade#position">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Current open positions are shown in this part. By default, only brief information about positions is shown here. To see more details, tap once on the position line.</span></p><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Position" title="Position" width="731" height="315" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/position_details.png"></p><ol><li value="1" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Symbol</span><span class="f_ol"> — a financial instrument of the open position.</span></li><li value="2" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Type</span><span class="f_ol"> — position type: "Buy" — long, "Sell" — short.</span></li><li value="3" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Volume</span><span class="f_ol"> — volume of a trade operation (in lots or units). The minimal volume and its change step are limited by a brokerage company, the maximal value depends on the deposit size.</span></li><li value="4" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">#</span><span class="f_ol"> — position ticket (unique number).</span></li><li value="5" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Price</span><span class="f_ol"> — the price of a deal, as a result of which the position was opened. If the opened position is a result of execution of several deals, then this field displays their weighted average price: (price of deal 1 * volume of deal 1 + ... + price of deal N * volume of deal N) / (volume of deal 1 + ... + volume of deal N). The accuracy of rounding of the the weighted average price is equal to the number of decimal places in the symbol price plus three additional characters.</span></li><li value="6" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Price</span><span class="f_ol"> — the current price of the financial symbol.</span></li><li value="7" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Profit</span><span class="f_ol"> — the financial result of the deal execution, taking into account the current price. A positive result indicates the profit, a negative value means the loss.</span></li><li value="8" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Δ</span><span class="f_ol"> — the difference (delta) between the open price and the current price in points and in percentage terms.</span></li><li value="9" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">S/L</span><span class="f_ol"> — the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#stop_loss" class="topiclink">Stop Loss</a> level of the current position. If the order has not been placed, a dash is displayed.</span></li><li value="10" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">T/P</span><span class="f_ol"> — the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#take_profit" class="topiclink">Take Profit</a> level of the current position. If the order has not been placed, a dash is displayed.</span></li><li value="11" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Swap</span><span class="f_ol"> — amount of swaps charged.</span></li><li value="12" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Time</span><span class="f_ol"> — time when the position was opened. The record is represented as YYYY.MM.DD HH:MM (year.month.day hour:minute).</span></li><li value="13" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Comment</span><span class="f_ol"> — a comment to a position. A comment can only be added when <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/open_positions" class="topiclink">placing an order</a> from the PC version of the MetaTrader 5 platform. The comment cannot be changed when modifying an order. Also a comment on a trade operation can be added by a brokerage company.</span></li></ol><h2><a name="orders" class="hmanchor"></a>Orders <a class="anchor" href="trade#orders">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Existing pending orders are shown in this section. By default, only brief information about orders is shown here. To see more details, tap once on the order line.</span></p><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Order" title="Order" width="731" height="327" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/order_details.png"></p><ol><li value="1" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Symbol</span><span class="f_ol"> — the financial instrument of the pending order.</span></li><li value="2" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Type</span><span class="f_ol"> — <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#pending_order" class="topiclink">type of the pending order</a>: "Sell Stop", "Sell Limit", "Buy Stop", "Buy Limit", "Buy Stop Limit" or "Sell Stop Limit".</span></li><li value="3" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">#</span><span class="f_ol"> — ticket number (a unique identifier) of the pending order.</span></li><li value="4" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Price</span><span class="f_ol"> — the current price of the financial symbol.</span></li><li value="5" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Price</span><span class="f_ol"> — price reaching which the pending order will trigger.</span></li><li value="6" class="p_ol"><a name="state" class="hmanchor"></a><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">State</span><span class="f_ol"> — the current <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_state" class="topiclink">status</a> of the pending order: "Started", "Placed" etc.</span></li><li value="7" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Volume</span><span class="f_ol"> — volume requested in the pending order, and volume covered by the deal (in lots or units).</span></li><li value="8" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">S/L</span><span class="f_ol"> — level of the placed <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#stop_loss" class="topiclink">Stop Loss order</a>. If the order has not been placed, a dash is displayed.</span></li><li value="9" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">T/P</span><span class="f_ol"> — level of the set <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#take_profit" class="topiclink">Take Profit</a> order. If the order has not been placed, a dash is displayed.</span></li><li value="10" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Time</span><span class="f_ol"> — pending order placing time. The record is represented as YYYY.MM.DD HH:MM (year.month.day hour:minute).</span></li><li value="11" class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Comment</span><span class="f_ol"> — a comment to the order. A comment can only be added when <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/pending_place" class="topiclink">placing an order</a> from the PC version of the MetaTrader 5 platform. The comment cannot be changed when modifying an order.
