# Push Notifications

The MetaTrader 5 app for iPhone supports [push notifications](/en/docs/mt5/iphone/push). Push notifications are short text messages that can be sent to your device from the PC version of the [trading platform](https://www.metatrader5.com/en/terminal/help/startworking/settings#notifications), and from various services of the [MQL5.community](https://www.mql5.com/ "MQL5.community").

Push notifications are very important. This option allows traders to keep abreast of the latest updates. An Expert Advisor running in a desktop platform can notify a trader about the emergence of a new signal. The trader receives the a notification in the mobile device and can make a trading operation through the MetaTrader 5 for iPhone.

During installation of MetaTrader 5 for iPhone, a unique identifier called [MetaQuotes ID](/en/docs/mt5/iphone/settings_messages#my_id) is assigned to each user. This identifier is used like a phone number. Knowing it, various applications can send messages to the user.

Push notifications are received by the device regardless of whether the MetaTrader 5 application is running or not. All that is needed is the cellular connection.

Push messages are displayed in the notification center of your device:

![Messages in the notification center](/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/messages_new_notify.png "Messages in the notification center")

The entire [history of push notifications](/en/docs/mt5/iphone/settings_messages) is stored in the MetaTrader 5 for iPhone and can be viewed at any time:

![The History of Messages](/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/message_history.png "The History of Messages")
