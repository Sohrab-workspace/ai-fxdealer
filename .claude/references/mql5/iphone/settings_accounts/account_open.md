<table class="container" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0" border="0"><tbody><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><h1>Demo Account Opening</h1><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The MetaTrader 5 app for iPhone supports two types of accounts: demonstration (demo) and real.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Demo accounts provide the opportunity to work in a training mode without real money, allowing to test a trading strategy. They have the same functionality as the real ones. The difference is that demo accounts can be opened without any investment and, therefore, one cannot expect to profit from them.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To open a demo account, tap <img class="help" alt="New Account" title="New Account" width="20" height="20" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/account_add_icon.png"> on the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/settings_accounts" class="topiclink">account management</a> page. Next, select the server (brokerage company) on which you wish to open a demo account.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">By default, the list does not display all available servers. To find the desired broker server, start typing its name into the search bar.</span></p><div><div class="parent-table"><table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable"><ul><li class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">Search results are displayed after you type three characters.</span></li><li class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">Contact your broker to know the server name.</span></li></ul></td></tr></tbody></table></div></div><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Tap on the desired server line and then tap "Demo account".</span></p><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Server selection and switching to account registration" title="Server selection and switching to account registration" width="651" height="582" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/new_account_server_type.png"></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h2>Account Parameters</h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Specify the following account parameters here:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">First Name, Last Name</span><span class="f_ol"> — the full name of the user, consisting of at least eight characters.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Mobile</span><span class="f_ol"> — mobile phone number in international format. For example, +496912345678. Please be sure to indicate your mobile phone number. Landline phone numbers are not accepted.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">E-mail</span><span class="f_ol"> — e-mail address, for example: "john@smith.com".</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Use hedge</span><span class="f_ol"> — enable this option to open an account with the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept#hedging" class="topiclink">hedging position accounting system</a>, which allows having multiple open positions of the same symbol simultaneously, including positions in opposite directions. Otherwise, an account with the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept#netting" class="topiclink">netting position accounting system</a> will be opened for you. Types of account that are available to you depend on the broker.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Account Type</span><span class="f_ol"> — select the type of accounts supported by the broker.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Leverage</span><span class="f_ol"> — ratio between borrowed and owned funds for trading.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Deposit</span><span class="f_ol"> — initial deposit in the basic currency.</span></li></ul></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Account Parameters" title="Account Parameters" width="324" height="647" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/demo_account_param.png"></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To open an account, tap the "Register" button at the top of the window.</span></p></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Completion of Account Registration" title="Completion of Account Registration" width="324" height="648" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/demo_account_done.png"></p></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><h2>Completion of Account Registration</h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">After some time a new demo account will be allocated to you. Its parameters will be displayed as shown above.</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Login</span><span class="f_ol"> — the number of the opened account.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><a name="main_password" class="hmanchor"></a><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Password</span><span class="f_ol"> — a password to access the account. This is a master password, which allows to perform trade operations on this account.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Investor</span><span class="f_ol"> — investor password. It allows you to connect using this account, view its state and analyze price dynamics, but it does not allow trading.</span></li></ul><div><div class="parent-table"><table class="attentable" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="8" border="1"><tbody><tr class="attentable"><td class="attentable" style="vertical-align:middle;"><ul><li class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">If you have any problems with account registration, please contact your broker's technical support team.</span></li><li class="p_tableatten"><span class="f_tableatten">If extended authorization mode is used for demo accounts, a special security certificate will be automatically generated and installed when opening an account. More information can be found in <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/settings_accounts/extended_authorization" class="topiclink">"Extended authentication"</a>.</span></li></ul></td></tr></tbody></table></div></div></td></tr><tr class="container"><td class="container" colspan="2" style="width:50%;"><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Using the "Copy to clipboard" button, you can copy the parameters of the opened account to clipboard in order to save them in any other application.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To complete the account opening procedure and to connect to the newly created account, tap "Start" at the bottom of the screen.</span></p></td></tr></tbody></table>
