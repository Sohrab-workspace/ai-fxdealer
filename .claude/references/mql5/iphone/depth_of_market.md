In this case, the Depth of Market displays price levels calculated based on the Bid and Ask prices using the price change step.</span></li></ul></td><td class="container" style="width:50%;"><p There are two types of trade operations:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;"><a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/depth_of_market#market" class="topiclink">Market Operations</a></span><span class="f_ol"> — buying/selling a financial instrument at the current market price;</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;"><a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/depth_of_market#request" class="topiclink">Trade Requests</a></span><span class="f_ol"> — placing various requests (<a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#pending_order" class="topiclink">pending orders</a>) to buy/sell a financial instrument at a specified price.</span></li></ul><h3><a name="market" class="hmanchor"></a>Market Operations <a class="anchor" href="depth_of_market#market">#</a></h3><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">A market operation is buying/selling a financial instrument at the best price currently offered on the market. No additional confirmation is needed.</span></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">If an account uses the <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept#hedging" class="topiclink">hedging mode</a>, a user can have multiple open positions of the same instrument. Therefore, to close a position from the Depth of Market, it must be explicitly selected. Only after that the "Close" button will become active.</span></p><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="If hedging is enabled, to close a position you need to select it first" title="If hedging is enabled, to close a position you need to select it first" width="650" height="550" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/dom_close_hedge.png"></p><h2><a name="request" class="hmanchor"></a>Trade Requests <a class="anchor" href="depth_of_market#request">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Placing a trader request means creating a <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#pending_order" class="topiclink">pending order</a> to buy/sell a financial instrument at a specified price. <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types" class="topiclink">4 types of pending orders</a>: Buy Limit, Sell Limit, Buy Stop and Sell Stop can be placed from the Depth of Market. The conventional way of performing <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/open_positions" class="topiclink">trade operations</a> should be used to place Buy Stop Limit and Sell Stop Limit orders.</span></p><h3>Placing an Order</h3><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">To place a pending order, tap on the bid/ask volume cell having a required price:</span></p><p class="p_Image"><img class="help" alt="Placing pending orders via the Depth of Market" title="Placing pending orders via the Depth of Market" width="540" height="552" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/dom_trade_pending.png"></p><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">After you tap on the appropriate field, a request for placing a pending order will be immediately sent to the server. The order will be displayed in the Depth of Market upon successful placement.</span><span class="f_ol"> As soon as there is a market participant ready to sell the financial instrument at the specified price, the order will be filled and will turn into a <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept#position" class="topiclink">position</a>.</span></p><h3>Transferring Orders to an External Trading System</h3><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt"><a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types" class="topiclink">Pending orders</a> may or may not be transferred to an external trading system depending on broker's symbol settings. For example, a broker may transfer Limit orders directly to an external trading system while other order types (Stop and Stop Limit ones) will be processed in MetaTrader 5:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol">An appropriate <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/depth_of_market#market" class="topiclink">market operation is executed</a> when the stop price specified in a Buy Stop or Sell Stop order is reached.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol">When the stop price specified in a Buy Stop Limit or Sell Stop Limit order is reached, an appropriate limit request is executed, which will be visible to other market participants.</span></li></ul></td></tr></tbody></table></div></div><h2><a name="pending" class="hmanchor"></a>Interface: Current Position <a class="anchor" href="depth_of_market#pending">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The name of the trading instrument is displayed at the top of the Depth of Market. If there are any open positions for this symbol, an arrow will be displayed next to its name. Tap on the arrow to view information about positions:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol">Ticket (hedging) or instrument name (netting)</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol">Position direction — Buy or Sell</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol">Open price and current price</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol">Profit/loss</span></li></ul><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Lots/Amount button allows you to switch quotes volume display mode in the Depth of Market.</span></p><h2>Interface: List of Requests</h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The main part of DOM is occupied by the list of trade requests which are closest to the market. The upper part (colored in red) displays bids, while the lower part (colored in blue)</span><span class="f_ol"> displays asks. List of requests is divided into three columns:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Volume</span><span class="f_ol"> — total volume of requests at a specified price</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Price</span><span class="f_ol"> — request price</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Trading</span><span class="f_ol"> — this column displays trader's current trade requests and stop levels (<a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#stop_loss" class="topiclink">Stop Loss</a> and <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#take_profit" class="topiclink">Take Profit</a>). For example:</span></li></ul><ul><li class="p_ol_2lev"><span class="f_ol_2lev">BL 10 — Buy Limit order of 10 lots</span></li><li class="p_ol_2lev"><span class="f_ol_2lev">SL 5 — Sell Limit order of 5 lots</span></li><li class="p_ol_2lev"><span class="f_ol_2lev">BS 1 — Buy Stop order of 1 lot</span></li><li class="p_ol_2lev"><span class="f_ol_2lev">SS 3 — Sell Stop order of 3 lots</span></li><li class="p_ol_2lev"><span class="f_ol_2lev">sl 1 — a 1-lot Stop Loss level of a position</span></li><li class="p_ol_2lev"><span class="f_ol_2lev">tp 2 — a 2-lot Take Profit level of a position</span></li></ul><h2><a name="trade" class="hmanchor"></a>Interface: Trading Commands <a class="anchor" href="depth_of_market#trade">#</a></h2><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Traders can send market and limit (<a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#pending_order" class="topiclink">Buy Limit</a> and <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/general_concept/order_types#pending_order" class="topiclink">Sell Limit</a>) orders.</span></p><h3>Pending Orders</h3><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Buttons for placing pending orders are located at the right side of Trading column. The order is placed at the price with a pressed button.</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><img class="help" alt="Set Buy Limit/Buy Stop" title="Set Buy Limit/Buy Stop" width="25" height="25" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/dom_buy_limit_button.png"><span class="f_ol"> — if this button is pressed in the Bid prices area, a Buy Limit order at a specified price will be placed; in case it is pressed in the Ask prices area, a Buy Stop order will be placed;</span></li><li class="p_ol"><img class="help" alt="Set Sell Limit/Sell Stop" title="Set Sell Limit/Sell Stop" width="25" height="25" src="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/img/dom_sell_limit_button.png"><span class="f_ol"> — if this button is pressed in the Ask prices area, a Sell Limit order at a specified price will be placed; in case it is pressed in the Bid prices area, a Sell Stop order will be placed.</span></li></ul><h3>Trading Settings</h3><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">The upper part of DOM shows trade operation settings:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">sl</span><span class="f_ol"> — Stop Loss level is set for placed limit orders. This parameter is specified in points from the current price.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">vol</span><span class="f_ol"> — the volume of created market and limit orders in lots.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">tp</span><span class="f_ol"> — Take Profit level is set for placed limit orders. This parameter is specified in points from the current price.</span></li></ul><h3>Market Orders</h3><p class="p_txt"><span class="f_txt">Market order commands are located below:</span></p><ul><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Sell</span><span class="f_ol"> — perform a Sell deal in the size specified in "vol" field at the best available price.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Close</span><span class="f_ol"> — this button is only enabled if there is an <a href="/en/docs/mt5/iphone/trade/open_positions" class="topiclink">open position</a> for a selected financial instrument. It allows you to close the entire position at the best available price.</span></li><li class="p_ol"><span class="f_ol" style="font-weight: bold;">Buy</span><span class="f_ol"> — perform a Buy deal in the size specified in "vol" field at the best available price.</span></li></ul></td></tr></tbody></table>
