# The guide for installing, configuring and working with MetaTrader 5.

MetaTrader 5 is an advanced complete-cycle multi-asset trading platform for organizing brokerage services.

You traders can perform operations on different markets and exchanges using a single account. The flexible trading systems enables the highly efficient management of all asset types, including currencies, futures and stocks. In addition to essential trading features, you can offer professional technical and fundamental analysis tools to your traders, as well as the ability to use algorithmic trading robots.

The distributed platform architecture can handle high loads allowing companies to provide services to hundreds of thousands of online users. The system has built-in failover mechanisms and protection against possible external attacks.

With the advanced back-office system, you can organize the workflow to match your business expectations.

## Installation

Prepare hardware in accordance with the [system requirements](/en/docs/mt5/platform/platform_installation/requirements). We recommend using at least two dedicated servers hosted by different providers to implement the main and the backup infrastructures. [Prepare the system](/en/docs/mt5/platform/platform_installation/installation_preparations) and proceed to MetaTrader 5 [installation](/en/docs/mt5/platform/platform_installation/installation). After that, [activate the license](/en/docs/mt5/platform/platform_installation/activation) and configure your platform.

Use the [quick deployment](/en/docs/mt5/platform/platform_installation/deployer) feature to install additional platform components. If you need to move the platform or reconfigure the network infrastructure, use [console commands](/en/docs/mt5/platform/platform_installation/console_setup).

## Platform Setup

MetaTrader 5 trading platform administration tasks cover the management of trading servers and of all intermediate components (access points, backup servers, history data servers, etc.). The relevant setup data is provided in the appropriate sections.

Before you proceed to platform configuration, please read about the [basic trading concepts](/en/docs/mt5/platform/administration/common_info/general_concept) and underlying principles explaining the [arrangement of price charts and Depth of Market features](/en/docs/mt5/platform/administration/common_info/price_data).

## Platform Components

The MetaTrader 5 trading platform is a distributed system. The main functions are implemented on different components to enable efficient load balancing, allowing companies to offer services to hundreds of thousands traders.

The components interact via the main trading server which stores all platform settings.
